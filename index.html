Mental Health Support Group

(Inform & Analyze)

Welcome to Infyze! We’re a group of students from APEC Schools Sta. Rita, here to support your mental health. We know that in places like Sitio Bebe, where many face poverty and diseases like respiratory infections, where stress and anxiety can be overwhelming. Infyze offers a safe space for people to talk, share their struggles, and get advice on managing these challenges. We’re here to help reduce the stress and offer support to those who need it.
