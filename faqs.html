Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Why is mental health important?
A: Mental health is a big part of who we are. It shapes how we think, feel, and handle the ups and downs of life. Whether it’s managing stress, building good relationships, or making decisions, taking care of our mental health helps us live a balanced and fulfilling life.
2. How can I access Infyze’s services?
A: Infyze is always here for you, 24/7. Whether you’re having a tough day or just need someone to talk to, you can reach out through our website. We’ve made it easy to get support whenever and wherever you need it.
3. Who can use Infyze?
A: Infyze is open to anyone in need of mental health support. Whether you’re seeking guidance, a listening ear, or professional help, Infyze is here for you.
