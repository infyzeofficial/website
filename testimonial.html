Our Journey!

Infyze started as a school project, but it became something really close to us.
We made YouTube videos where we interviewed students about how they handle stress,
and talked about how social media affects mental health.
Places like in Sitio Bebe, we’ve seen how hard life can get—and we
just wanted to create a space where people feel heard and less alone.
We're just students, but we wanted to make a difference, even in the smallest way.
